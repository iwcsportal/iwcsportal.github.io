Hello School Board, I hope you find this site helpful as you navigate various technologies. You can always return to this site by clicking on "School Board Hub" at the bottom of the KCS website. If you already have a login, just click "Enter the School Board Hub."

Technology Help

  1. Please use Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome. Safari on Mac has been known to cause issues with these resources.
  2. Recommended: Download for Microsoft Teams.
  3. Recommended: Download the Microsoft Outlook mobile App for iOS or Android.

Passwords

You may choose to access Microsoft Teams using either your "Guest Access Email" or your "KCS Issued Email Address." Both of these accounts allow you to reset your password on your own.

  1. Go to https://myapps.microsoft.com/kingswayschool.onmicrosoft.com.
  2. Enter your Guest Access email or KCS Issued email and click next.
  3. If you are using a Gmail address, you may just be prompted to login with Gmail and then you'll be done.
  4. If you don't know your password or were never given one, click “Forgot my password.”
  5. You may be required to enter various security prompts such as characters displayed on the screen or codes sent to your email or phone number.
